Before writing parsers, define the platform.

Because if this succeeds, the parser will become less than 5% of the total system. The real asset will be the enterprise knowledge model and reasoning layer built on top of it.

That is where cross-organization value emerges. That's the part that can survive changes in COBOL dialects, repositories, vendors, and modernization strategies.
