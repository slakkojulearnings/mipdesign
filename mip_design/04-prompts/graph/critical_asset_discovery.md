# Critical Asset Discovery

> Prompt 23 · Category: Graph · Skills: [graph-engineer](../../03-skills/graph-engineer/SKILL.md), [resilience-engineer](../../03-skills/resilience-engineer/SKILL.md)
> Honors [MIP Engineering Principles](../../03-skills/MIP_ENGINEERING_PRINCIPLES.md).

## Purpose
Identify assets that represent the highest business and technical risk.

## Inputs
Knowledge Graph · Call Graph · Batch Graph · Lineage Graph · Runtime Evidence.

## Instructions
**Analyze:** Centrality · Reachability · Execution Frequency · Business Capability Mapping · Dependency Density.
**Rank:** Programs · Jobs · Tables · Datasets · Transactions.

## Expected Output
- Critical Asset Catalog
- Risk Rankings
- Modernization Priority Matrix

## Constraints
Rankings derived from graph evidence; each carries a confidence score.

## Success Criteria
Business-critical assets are objectively identified.
