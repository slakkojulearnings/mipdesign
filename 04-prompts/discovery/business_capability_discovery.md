# Business Capability Discovery

> Prompt 05B · Category: Discovery · Skill: [business-capability-analyst](../../03-skills/business-capability-analyst/SKILL.md)
> Honors [MIP Engineering Principles](../../03-skills/MIP_ENGINEERING_PRINCIPLES.md).

## Purpose
Discover business capabilities embedded within the application landscape.

## Context
Modernization should align with business capabilities rather than technical boundaries.

## Inputs
Programs · Jobs · Copybooks · Documentation.

## Instructions
**Apply:** Semantic Analysis · Naming Pattern Analysis · Functional Clustering · Dependency Clustering.

**Identify:** Business Domains · Business Capabilities · Capability Ownership · Capability Dependencies.

**Generate:** Capability Map · Domain Map · Capability-to-System Matrix.

## Expected Output
- Business Capability Catalog
- Domain Architecture
- Capability Dependency Analysis

## Constraints
Every capability assignment carries supporting evidence and a confidence score; low-confidence assignments are flagged for review.

## Success Criteria
Business capabilities become visible and traceable.

## Review Checklist
- Capabilities identified from evidence?
- Domains mapped?
- Confidence scores attached and low-confidence flagged?
