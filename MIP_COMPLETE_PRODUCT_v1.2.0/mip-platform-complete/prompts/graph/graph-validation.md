# Purpose

Validate graph integrity and quality.

# Prompt

Check ontology-valid edge combinations, missing nodes, placeholder/unresolved ratios, duplicate edges, self-loops, cycles, disconnected components, evidence coverage, confidence distribution, and contradictory relationships. Separate warnings from failures and produce remediation actions.

# Required Output

- assumptions
- evidence used
- proposed or completed work
- tests and validation
- unknowns and risks
- next executable step

# Completion Rule

Do not declare completion until the stated acceptance criteria or validation commands pass. Follow `CLAUDE.md` and the relevant skill.
