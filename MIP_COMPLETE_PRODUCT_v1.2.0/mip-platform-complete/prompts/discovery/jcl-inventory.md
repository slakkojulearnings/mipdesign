# Purpose

Build the job and procedure inventory.

# Prompt

Analyze JCL, PROCs, scheduler exports, and control cards. Extract jobs, procedures, steps, executed programs, utilities, DD datasets, DISP semantics, conditions, parameters, restart behavior, upstream/downstream evidence, issues, and confidence. Keep unresolved symbols explicit.

# Required Output

- assumptions
- evidence used
- proposed or completed work
- tests and validation
- unknowns and risks
- next executable step

# Completion Rule

Do not declare completion until the stated acceptance criteria or validation commands pass. Follow `CLAUDE.md` and the relevant skill.
