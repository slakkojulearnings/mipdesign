# Purpose

Design a graph feature or ontology change.

# Prompt

Act as a knowledge-graph engineer. Define node types, edge types, source/target validity, dependency direction, evidence, confidence, traversal semantics, cycle handling, unresolved nodes, persistence mapping, NetworkX implementation, test cases, and export behavior. Do not infer business criticality from centrality alone.

# Required Output

- assumptions
- evidence used
- proposed or completed work
- tests and validation
- unknowns and risks
- next executable step

# Completion Rule

Do not declare completion until the stated acceptance criteria or validation commands pass. Follow `CLAUDE.md` and the relevant skill.
